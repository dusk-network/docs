## Operator workflow

1. [Choose a network](/operator/networks/) and confirm its endpoints and chain ID.
2. Choose the appropriate [provisioner](/operator/provisioner/), [archive](/operator/archive-node/), or [prover](/operator/prover/) role.
3. Follow the role-specific installation and configuration instructions.
4. Configure the [node wallet](/operator/guides/node-wallet-setup/) when the role requires one.
5. Establish [maintenance and monitoring](/operator/maintenance-monitoring/) before treating the node as production infrastructure.
